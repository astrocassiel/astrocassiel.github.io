Internship Experiences

Roles in aerospace, government, and research labs that shaped my journey in advanced manufacturing.

Internships & Appointments

NASA Marshall Space Flight Center (Summer 2025)

Applied two-color thermography for melt pool monitoring on a Cold Metal Transfer WAAM system. Assisted with process monitoring on other DED systems using regolith simulants.

  • In-Situ Thermal Monitoring
  • Tech Transfer
  • WAAM + Regolith

The Aerospace Corporation (Summer 2022)

Captured in-situ temperature data on powder-fed DED parts to study porosity and hardness. Conducted metallography and correlated microstructure with thermal signatures.

  • DED Process Monitoring
  • Metallography
  • Hardness Correlation

Launcher (acquired by VAST) – Summer 2021

Operated VELO3D LPBF machine and designed thermal channels for the Orbiter engine. Created PLA gimbal mockups for payload deployment.

  • VELO3D LPBF Operations
  • Orbiter Engine Design
  • Thermal Channel Optimization

KCNSC Honeywell FM&T – Summer 2020

Designed fastening tools for LPBF manufacturing and evaluated surface roughness changes with profilometry. Co-authored AM machinability documentation.

  • LPBF Tool Design
  • Deburr & Profilometry
  • Process Documentation

MIT Lincoln Laboratory (Spring 2022)

Assisted in CubeSat deployment hardware testing under thermal extremes. Created and validated designs for launch survivability.

  • CubeSat Design
  • Thermal Testing
  • SolidWorks CAD

NASA Glenn Research Center (Summer 2019)

Analyzed thermal performance of propulsion components and contributed to safety documentation for manned space missions.

  • Propulsion Systems
  • Thermal Analysis
  • Risk Evaluation

UTEP Keck Center (2019–2021)

Researched DIW printing of soft actuators and metal-filled filaments for post-sintered metal parts. Investigated FDM parameter effects on density and porosity.

  • Soft Robotics
  • Direct Ink Writing
  • FDM Metal Filaments
