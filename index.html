About Me

I'm Gala Cassiel Solis, a PhD candidate at Carnegie Mellon University researching advanced metal additive manufacturing techniques. My work focuses on in-situ thermal monitoring and closed-loop process control for WAAM systems, with an emphasis on enhancing mechanical performance and reducing defects. I’ve collaborated with NASA, the Army Research Lab, and industry partners to translate research into applied aerospace manufacturing.
